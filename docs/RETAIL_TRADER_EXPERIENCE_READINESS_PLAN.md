## Why Implementation Is Not Allowed Yet

Retail Trader Experience is closer to a user-facing decision surface than the Retail Dashboard skeletons. It must first define personas, use-case placeholders, experience sections, API/display contracts, safety labels, no-recommendation policy, no-execution policy, and boundary audit checks. Until those prompts complete, it cannot create active UI, recommendation cards, broker controls, or execution paths.
