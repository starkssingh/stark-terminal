# Retail Trader Experience Planning Milestone Audit

Prompt 60 audits Prompt 56 Retail Trader Experience Planning and Guardrails as
part of the Prompts 56-59 milestone review.

## Planning Contracts Status

Retail Trader Experience planning contracts remain planning and guardrails
only. They define future experience intent, stage, planned personas, journeys,
sections, cards, forbidden interactions, safety flags, and unavailable-by-
default behavior.

## Placeholder Status

- Persona placeholders remain placeholders and are not suitability profiles.
- Journey placeholders remain placeholders and are not trading advice.
- Section placeholders remain placeholders and are not active decision screens.
- Card placeholders remain placeholders and are not recommendation cards.
- Reference placeholders remain placeholders and are not active dashboard,
  active DecisionObject, safety pass, approval, override, broker, or execution
  references.

## Forbidden Interaction Status

The forbidden interaction registry remains complete for this phase. It covers
recommendation cards, action buttons, confidence scores, DecisionObject
display, readiness-to-trade badges, broker controls, order buttons, approval
controls, override controls, suitability profiling, and live data controls.

## Safety And Readiness Helpers Status

Safety helpers remain fail-closed. Readiness helpers do not permit active UI,
recommendations, action generation, confidence scoring, DecisionObject
generation, readiness-to-trade, broker controls, suitability profiling, or
execution.

## Milestone Verdict

Pass. Planning remains planning/guardrails only. There is no active UI, no
frontend implementation, no desktop implementation, no recommendation cards,
no suitability profiling, no broker controls, no approval/override controls,
and no execution controls.
