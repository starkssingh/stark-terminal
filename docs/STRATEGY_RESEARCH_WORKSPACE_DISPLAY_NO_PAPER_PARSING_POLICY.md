# Strategy Research Workspace Display No Paper Parsing Policy

Prompt 65 does not implement paper ingestion or paper parsing display behavior.

## Policy

- No paper ingestion display.
- No paper parsing display.
- No PDF parsing.
- No arXiv ingestion.
- No method extraction display.
- No strategy extraction display.
- No code generation from paper.
- No backtest generation from paper.
- No recommendation generation from paper.

Paper references are visual placeholders only. The Strategy Research Workspace
Display is unavailable by default and display contract skeleton only. It adds
no active UI, no frontend components, no desktop components, no strategy
generation, no strategy code generation, no backtesting, no optimization, no
confidence scoring, no DecisionObject generation, no readiness-to-trade, no
broker controls, and no execution APIs.

Development remains Mac mini M2 / macOS / Apple Silicon. The target desktop
product remains Windows-native Stark Terminal.
