# Research Artifact Index No Search Ranking Policy

Prompt 77 does not introduce a search engine, ranking engine, retrieval engine, semantic search, keyword search, ranking/scoring, or performance claims.

## Prohibited

- No search_artifacts function.
- No rank_artifacts function.
- No retrieve_artifacts function.
- No semantic_search function.
- No keyword_search function.
- No ranking scores.
- No ranking weights.
- No search endpoint.
- No ranking endpoint.
- No retrieval endpoint.

Tags and keys are inert placeholders only. They cannot produce recommendations, action states, confidence scores, DecisionObjects, readiness-to-trade, broker controls, or execution APIs.
