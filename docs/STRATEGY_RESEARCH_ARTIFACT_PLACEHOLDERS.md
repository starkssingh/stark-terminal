# Strategy Research Artifact Placeholders

Strategy Research artifact placeholders define future artifact metadata only.

## Planned Artifact Kinds

- Paper reference.
- Strategy hypothesis.
- Dataset reference.
- Experiment plan.
- Evidence placeholder.
- Safety note.

## Boundary

Artifacts are not validated strategies. They are not parsed papers, not backtest-ready artifacts, not recommendation-ready artifacts, and not execution-ready artifacts. They do not create active UI, strategy generation, backtesting, recommendation generation, confidence scoring, DecisionObject generation, broker controls, or execution APIs.
