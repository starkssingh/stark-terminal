# Research Artifact Lifecycle Placeholders

These are Research Artifact Registry planning and guardrails documents.

ResearchArtifactLifecyclePlaceholder supports PLACEHOLDER, REFERENCED, DRAFT,
REVIEW_REQUIRED, BLOCKED, DEFERRED, and UNKNOWN enum values, while active
placeholder instances reject UNKNOWN and do not imply validated strategy,
approved strategy, recommendation, readiness-to-trade, or execution.

Lifecycle placeholders are not approval workflows. They do not unlock active
artifact ingestion, active artifact storage, persistent artifact storage, paper
parsing, PDF parsing, arXiv ingestion, strategy generation, backtesting,
optimization, recommendations, broker controls, approvals, overrides, or
execution APIs.

Development remains on Mac mini M2 / macOS / Apple Silicon. The target desktop
product remains Windows-native Stark Terminal.
