# Strategy Research Boundary No Backtesting Policy

Prompt 68 keeps backtesting behavior boundary-hardening-only and forbidden.

Development remains Mac mini M2 / macOS / Apple Silicon. The target desktop
product remains Windows-native Stark Terminal.

The boundary layer does not add a backtesting engine, backtest result
endpoints, optimization, parameter search, walk-forward analysis, performance
claims, strategy validation, strategy-to-backtest paths, live trading
controls, or paper trading controls.

Experiment artifacts remain non-executable placeholders only. Future
backtesting requires a future prompt and audit-before-unlock.
