# Strategy Research Workspace Placeholders

Strategy Research Workspace placeholders define future workspace surfaces without rendering or activating them.

## Planned Workspace Kinds

- Paper research placeholder.
- Strategy hypothesis placeholder.
- Dataset context placeholder.
- Experiment plan placeholder.
- Evidence review placeholder.
- Safety review placeholder.

## Boundary

The placeholders are planning-only and unavailable-by-default. They create no active UI, no frontend components, no desktop components, no paper parsing, no strategy generation, no backtesting, no recommendation behavior, no confidence scoring, no DecisionObject generation, no readiness-to-trade, no broker controls, and no execution behavior.
