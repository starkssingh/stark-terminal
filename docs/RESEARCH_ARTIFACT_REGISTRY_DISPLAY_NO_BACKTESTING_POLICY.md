# Research Artifact Registry Display No Backtesting Policy

Prompt 72 display contracts cannot run or display backtests.

Forbidden behavior includes backtesting, optimization, parameter search, walk-forward analysis, performance claims, benchmark results, backtest metrics, backtest result endpoints, strategy validation, and live or paper trading controls.

Lifecycle badge placeholders cannot use meanings such as BACKTESTED_PROFITABLE, VALIDATED_STRATEGY, READY_TO_TRADE, or EXECUTABLE.

Backtesting remains forbidden until a future prompt explicitly plans and audits the boundary.
