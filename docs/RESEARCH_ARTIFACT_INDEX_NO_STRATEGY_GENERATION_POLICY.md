# Research Artifact Index No Strategy Generation Policy

Prompt 77 does not introduce strategy generation, strategy code generation, signal generation, factor generation, alpha generation, generated thresholds, artifact-to-strategy paths, or paper-to-strategy paths.

## Prohibited

- No generate_strategy function.
- No generate_strategy_code function.
- No generate_signal function.
- No generate_factor function.
- No generate_alpha function.
- No artifact-to-strategy path.
- No paper-to-strategy path.
- No method extraction.
- No strategy extraction.

Index metadata, keys, references, tags, provenance, and lifecycle placeholders cannot become strategy outputs or executable instructions.
