# Research Artifact Index Display No Paper Parsing Policy

Prompt 79 forbids paper parsing through Research Artifact Index Display.

Display placeholders cannot show parsed paper content, parsed paper excerpts, extracted methods, extracted strategies, PDF parsing results, arXiv ingestion results, or LLM paper analysis output.

Policy:

- No paper parsing.
- No PDF parsing.
- No arXiv ingestion.
- No LLM paper analysis.
- No method extraction.
- No strategy extraction.
- No paper-to-code.
- No paper-to-backtest.
- No strategy generation.
- No backtesting.
- No execution APIs.
